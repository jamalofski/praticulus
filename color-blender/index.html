Color Blender

Blend two colors together and see intermediate shades. Adjust the number of midpoints to create smooth transitions.

What Is Color Blending?

Color blending creates intermediate colors between two endpoints by interpolating their RGB values. This is useful for creating smooth gradients, design systems with tonal scales, and finding the "middle ground" between two brand colors.

Each midpoint represents an equal step in the transition from Color 1 to Color 2. Click any color chip to copy its HEX code.
