CSS Gradient Types

Linear gradients transition colors along a straight line at a given angle. Perfect for backgrounds, overlays, and buttons.

Radial gradients radiate colors from a center point outward. Great for spotlight effects and circular patterns.

Conic gradients rotate colors around a center point. Useful for pie charts, color wheels, and creative backgrounds.
