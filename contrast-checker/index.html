WCAG Contrast Requirements

AA Normal text: Minimum ratio of 4.5:1. Applies to text smaller than 18pt (or 14pt bold).

AA Large text: Minimum ratio of 3:1. Applies to text 18pt+ (or 14pt+ bold).

AAA Normal text: Enhanced ratio of 7:1 for best readability.

AAA Large text: Enhanced ratio of 4.5:1 for large text.

Meeting these requirements ensures your content is readable by people with visual impairments, including color blindness and low vision.
