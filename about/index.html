About Praticulus

Fast, free, privacy-first tools for developers and everyone.

Why Praticulus?

We got tired of bloated tools with popups, mandatory signups, and trackers everywhere. Praticulus is different:

  • 100% client-side: Your data never leaves your browser. Verifiable in DevTools → Network
  • No signup: Every tool works immediately, no account needed
  • No tracking: No cookies, no analytics pixels, no fingerprinting
  • Fast: Lightweight pages with no heavy frameworks, holding a Lighthouse 100 budget on every tool
  • Free: All tools are free to use, forever

Read the full privacy policy for the technical details and how to verify the claims yourself.
